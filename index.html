FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

The 70-Day Academic Foundation Recovery Program is designed for students at all levels — from primary school to candidates preparing for WAEC, NECO, JAMB, or Post-UTME. If you feel your foundation is weak or you need intensive preparation, this program is for you.

You can register for as many subjects as you need. We cover 12 subjects including Mathematics, English, Sciences, Commercial subjects, and Art subjects. Our tutors will help you create a schedule that works for your needs.

We keep our classes small — typically 10-15 students per class. This ensures every student gets personal attention from the tutor and can ask questions freely.

Currently, our program is conducted in-person at our learning center. This allows for hands-on instruction, real-time feedback, and a focused learning environment.

Classes run Monday through Saturday with flexible time slots. We have morning and afternoon sessions to accommodate different schedules. The program runs for 70 days.

We conduct weekly assessments and provide detailed progress reports. You'll also have access to practice tests and mock exams that simulate real exam conditions.
